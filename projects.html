Music Genre Classification with Self-attention Mechanism

Music genre classification is a significant and practical field of Music Information Retrieval. Inspired by an advance in NLP, we have designed a transformer classifier. The transformer classifier analyzes the relationship between different audio time frames well.

  • Applied deep learning to Music Genre Classification tasks;

  • Designed a transformer classifier to classify music genre instead of traditional RNN model;

  • Used hierarchical topology consistently with the layering of music in the time and frequency domains.
